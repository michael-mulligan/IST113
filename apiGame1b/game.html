How to play:

Begin by picking a category and point value (higher is more difficult). You will be prompted with a clue. If you think you know the answer, you may type it in the text-field and click 'Submit'. If your answer is correct, the point value for the clue will be added to your points. If your answer is incorrect, the point value will be subtracted from your points. Finally, if you do not know the answer you may choose 'Skip' and you will be neither rewarded nor penalized. Answer all the clues and see how high of a score you can get!

Note: this game uses a primitive method to check your answers. Therefore, in addition to the game telling you if it thinks you are correct or incorrect, you will be prompted to make the final judgment for yourself. No cheating!
